SciCore Studios

We’re a team of 5 students tasked with developing and releasing a game for our high school entrepreneur course. Not only are we working towards getting a good grade, but we’re also determined to deliver an immersive, emotional and memorable experience to you all!

Sub-Profit

Sub-Profit is an immersive underwater rogue-like, primarily focusing on tension and paranoia. You will play as a submarine, with the goal of exploring procedurally generated cave systems to retrieve loot for upgrades. Avoid rogue and dangerous military sea-robots, or perhaps... deal with them.
